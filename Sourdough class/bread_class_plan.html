Gnocchi alla Sorrentina Friday night · serves 9–10

🍅 Tomato Sauce (make first)

1. Sauté 3 garlic cloves in generous olive oil over medium heat until golden, ~2 min.
2. Add 1.2kg passata. Season with salt. Simmer 20 min, stirring occasionally.
3. Add a large handful of torn basil at the end. Taste and adjust salt. Set aside.

🫙 Assemble the Dish

1. Boil gnocchi in well-salted water in batches. Pull them out 1 min early (al dente). Drain.
2. In a large baking dish: ladle sauce → gnocchi layer → torn mozzarella → more sauce → more mozzarella → Parmesan.
3. Finish with Parmesan on top. Drizzle olive oil. Cover with foil. Refrigerate overnight.
4. Saturday: bake covered at 180°C for 35 min. Uncover last 5 min to bubble & brown. Let rest 10 min before serving.
